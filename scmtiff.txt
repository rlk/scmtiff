
INVARIANT: SCM TIFF files are always BigTIFFs.

INVARIANT: SCM TIFF files always represent a complete, correct, usable spherical cube map file, regardless of data process progress.

 - The number of pages is 2^(2d+3)-2, even if most pages are empty.
 - The page size is n+2, even if the borders are unfilled.

scm-pds ....... Convert a NASA PDS file to a (leafy) SCM TIFF.
scm-merge ..... Combine multiple SCM TIFF files.
scm-sample .... Sample parent pages for an SCM TIFF wherever possible.
scm-border .... Fill the borders of all non-empty pages of an SCM TIFF.
scm-normal .... Generate a normal SCM TIFF from a height SCM TIFF.
